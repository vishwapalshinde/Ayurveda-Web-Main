Frequently Asked Questions

Panchakarma is a comprehensive Ayurvedic detoxification and rejuvenation therapy that aims to eliminate toxins from the body while balancing the doshas. It offers numerous benefits, including improved digestion, enhanced immunity, stress relief, and overall vitality.

Ayurveda is generally safe for most people. However, the suitability of specific treatments depends on age, health condition, and individual constitution. Consulting a qualified Ayurvedic doctor ensures personalized recommendations.

Results vary depending on the individual and the treatment plan. Some people notice improvements within days, while others may require several weeks of consistent Ayurvedic care for visible results.
